Suniti is a well known name in the field of Hair and Makeup who believes that ‘Makeup’ is not just a mask, but a perfect fusion of art, passion and experiment. Since 1991, she has earned admirable acknowledgment across the world for her significant contribution to the Hair and Makeup industry. She is one of those makeup artists who appreciated for her out of box thinking, creativity and good fashion sense. Her name is also registered in ‘Limca Book of Records 2011’ for doing the hair and makeup work of 9,000 performers in Commonwealth Games Delhi-2010. She also added beauty and elegance to the performers in first ever ‘Indian Formula 1 Grand Prix’ that took place in Greater Noida. Suniti also has a team of skilled and professional makeup artists who are serving thousands of clients every year across the world. She has also performed makeup of several prominent Bollywood and Sports personalities including Madhuri Dixit, Sushmita Sen, Aishwarya Rai, Deepika Padukone, Priyanka Chopra, Virat Kohli and Vijender Singh. She always strives to bring creativity in her work that helps to groom clients’ overall personality and make them stand out from the crowd. She dreamt of becoming a famous makeup artist at the age of five and since then, she never looked back. The journey of setting up the first makeup studio was full of challenges still she has overcome all the hurdles with her strong will and dedication. Based in Lajpat Nagar 2, Head Studio by Suniti also has a professional training institute that offers from Professional Makeup Courses & Sessions to Hair and Beauty services & training. Her studio’s presence can also be felt at Levo Ibis Hotel in Gurugram (formally known as Gurgaon). Her team of highly professional & creative background strives to perform modern, classical and trendy makeovers for all occasions. With their tons of experience and amazing fashion sense, each and every member of her team can glam you up for any occasion. So whether you want to look special and unique or have a desire to learn the real art of makeup then come to Makeup Studio and professional training institute by Suniti.
