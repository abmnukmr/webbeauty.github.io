ABOUT

Since its inception in the year 1991, Sunitismakers has emerged as one of the best professional make up studios in Delhi. To have a great name and fame in the make-up industry, the team of professionals has strived very well and achieved a great success. The experts have serviced to a number of clientele across the world such as “Formula 1 Grand Prix”, Hair and makeup of 9000 participants of Commonwealth Games 2010 which also helped them to get a record in Limca book of records 2011. The skillful professionals of the studio never disappoint their customers and make them always look beautiful. At Present, the professionals have decided to teach this art to the new-comers and make them best artists of their studios. With this vision, the owner has come up with an extensive range of different courses that potential students can opt as per their interests. These courses enable them to enhance their skill and make their career bright for better future. One thing that the expertise of the studio always shares with their client and customers is to become a skillful, dedicated and creative professional, it takes a lot of passion and time.
